Outlaw is a game about the fact that in our society, there has to be at least one who is guilty.

CONTROLS:

Player 1:
WASD to move and q for shooting

Player 2:
oklö to move and i for shooting

Goal:

Try to shoot the guilty one (red) without hurting one of the inoccents (white).
If you shoot innocents you become guilty too and the other player will get a reward for shooting you.
